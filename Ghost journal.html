Phantom

Evidence

  • Spirit Box
  • Fingerprints
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Abilities

1. Sometimes follows players outside the ghost room

2. During a hunt the Phantom "blinks" significantly slower than any other ghost

How to identify

1. During the Ghost Event take a photo of the ghost. If the ghost disappears - it is Phantom.

2. If you can't tell wether or not the ghost disappeared by itself look at the photo you took. If it says "Ghost" underneath, but you don't see a ghost on the photo - it is Phantom

3. During the hunt see if a ghost blinks very slowly. The minimum blink time for Phantom is 1 second(up to 2 seconds). That is it disappears for 1 second (or longer) during the blink

How to rule out

During the hunt if the blink is shorter than 1 second(0.3 seconds for normal ghosts) - It is not a Phantom

Hunts at

50% Sanity

Banshee

Evidence

  • Fingerprints
  • Ghost Orb
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Abilities

1. At a start of the game Banshee will pick a target and will do special stuff with it. It won't change the target untill the current target is dead

2. During the hunt if the target is in the house it will ignore any other players

3. Starts the hunt based on the target's sanity

4. Can leave the ghost room and walk towards it's target at any time

5. Banshee tends to perform a lot of singing ghost events. Does not apply to singing during the hunt

How to identify

1. After finding a ghost room walk far away from it. Set up a motion sensor and see if it triggers. If so, repeat the same thing with your teamate and see if it comes to them. If not - It is Banshee and you are the target

2. Use a Parabolic Microphone. If you hear paranormal screaming(30% chance to trigger it) - It is Banshee

How to rule out

If after 4-5 attemps of hearing screaming with Parabolic Microphone you didn't get it - It is most likely not Banshee (or you are just unlucky :D)

Hunts at

50% Sanity

Oni

Evidence

  • Freezing Temperatures
  • Ghost Orb
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Abilities

1. Oni tends to perform a lot more Ghost Events than the other ghosts

2. Oni is not able to perform an Air Ball Event (An orb flying towards you and a "swooosh-like" sound effect)

3. During the Ghost event Oni drops your sanity by 20% if you collide into him

4. During the hunt Oni is the most visible ghost. Its blinks are very short(0.1s - 0.5s)

1. Can be identified by the large amount of ghost events

2. During the Ghost event walk into the ghost, return to the truck and check if the sanity dropped by 20% (you need to know your initial sanity, obviously)

3. During the hunt see if the blinks are very short. Oni tends to be visible for most of the duration of the hunt

How to rule out

Can be ruled out whenever the Air Ball Event is triggered

Moroi

Evidence

  • Spirit Box
  • Ghost Writing
  • Freezing Temperatures

Abilities

1. The lower player's sanity, the faster Moroi is

2. If player's sanity is below 35% Moroi's speed is increased up to 2.25 m/s Don't forget that when it has a line of sight on you Moroi can speed up even more(up to 3.75 m/s), and be faster than Revenant

3. If you use a Spirit Box or Parabolic Microphone Moroi will put a curse on you which will start draining your sanity no matter what. The only way to get rid of curse is to use Sanity Pills

4. When using Smudge Sticks Moroi will be stunned by 12 seconds(6 for other ghosts)

How to identify

1. During hunt use Smudge Stick and count up to 6. If after 6 seconds a ghost is still stunned - It is Moroi

2. During hunt when looping ghost have Sanity Pills in your hand. After you see how fast the ghost is pop the pills and see if the speed changed. If so - It is Moroi

How to rule out

1. Can be ruled out if the Smudge Stick works for 6 seconds instead of 12

2. Can be ruled out if a ghost's speed is average even after player's sanity is below 35%

Hunts at

50% Sanity

Deogen

Evidence

  • Spirit Box
  • Ghost Writing
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Abilities

1. Deogen knows where the player is at all time. You cannot hide from it

2. Deogen moves at 3 m/s speed when the player is far away

3. When Deogen is close to a player its speed dropped down to 0.4 m/s (extremely slow). Can be easily looped

4. When using Spirit Box 1m away from Deogen it will give a special respond (loud breathing, 30% chance to trigger) instead of a regular answer

How to identify

1. During hunt check if the ghost is very fast untill it gets closer and very slow afterwards - If so - It is Deogen

2. When using Spirit Box check for the special respond(described above)

How to rule out

1. Can be ruled out if the speed of the ghost is not changing in a way described above

2. Can be ruled out if you was able to successfully hide from a ghost

Hunts at

40% Sanity

Thaye

Evidence

  • Ghost Orb
  • Ghost Writing
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Abilities

1. Thaye starts at a young age. Hunt can be initiated at 75% Sanity. When hunting at a young age Thaye's speed is 2.7 m/s (extremely high)

2. The longer player is in the room with Thaye the older she gets. Thus weakening her abilities

3. If a player stays for long period of time in a ghost room Thaye will get older and initiate hunt at 15% Sanity with a speed of 1 m/s

How to identify

1. Very agressive at the start of the game(tries to lower your sanity) and very passive at the further stages. So if you see the decrease in the speed over a period of time - It might be Thaye

2. Finding Evidence is the best way to identify Thaye

How to rule out

Can be ruled out if after 2-3 hunts didn't change speed and behavior

Hunts at

75% Sanity - Young age

15% Sanity - Old age
